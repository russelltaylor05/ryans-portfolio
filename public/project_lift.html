Machine Lift & Suspension System

Design, Prototype, and Testing of Hydraulic Lifting System

Description

The lifting system shown here is designed to lift a 3000lb conveying deck, and suspend it overhead in order to perform maintenance activites on machinery below. The system consists of hydraulic lifting cylinders, #80 chain, full width timing shaft, and impact resistant lifting yokes.

Due to spacial constraints, the chain path features a 3-to-1 stroke advantage in order to reduce cylinder length.

The full width timing shaft ensures equal lifting velocities across the machine and adds redundancy in the event of cylinder or chain failure.

The lifting yokes contain chain takeup lugs for leveling as well as bellville spring stacks which provide compliance in the event extreme acceleration or deceleration f the system.
